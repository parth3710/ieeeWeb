Unlock 15.0

It's an initiative under the IEEE Hyderabad Section Student Activities Committee and Computer Society Chapter IEEE Hyderabad Section which aims to enhance and bring out the coding skills of the participants to give their best at the IEEE Xtreme 15.O. 100+ participants will be selected based on their performances in 3 code contests in the month of August.

The selected participants will go through the scheduled program which includes

  • Workshops and webinars on different programming languages.
  • Xtreme awareness sessions.
  • Codeathons, hackathons and coding contests of different languages.
  • Quiz on coding logics and concepts.
  • Pre-Xtreme (a mock Xtreme)
  • Practice materials- previous Xtreme papers.
  • Mentorship for the participants in all the different aspects to make them be prepared for the IEEE Xtreme 15.O.

As a part of Unlock 15.O, there will be brainstorming sessions by eminent speakers from the industry who will help the participants to meet the needs of the industry as well as Xtreme 15.O The winners will be awarded a token of appreciation by the IEEE Hyderabad Section Computer Society Chapter and IEEE Hyderabad Section Student Activities Committee. Unlock 15.O is an attempt to boost the spirit of participants and withstand in today’s competitive world.
